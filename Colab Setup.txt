For those using Colab for the first time, follow the steps below to activate Colab in your Google account.

Follow below steps;

1. Open Google colab from https://colab.research.google.com/

2. Create a new Notebook.

3. In the "File" menu, click "Locate in Drive" option and navigate to your google drive folder, Done!

4. Now you can find the option to right click a notebook and select Google Colab option.
